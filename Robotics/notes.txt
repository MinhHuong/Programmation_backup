DEFINITION :
A robot : a machin that moves from location A  to B, has a goal to achieve, can sense, plan, act, programmable machine

CHARACTERISTICS :
- consistent (always pay attention)
- accurate
- reliable 
- do things that ppl cannot do (dangerous/risky/boring/dull job)
(dirtry dull dangerous --> jobs ppl dont want to do)

HUMANOID :
- create a machin that combines all necessary functions but also is affordable, reliable

- Robots can make ppl more strong (do strong things) (wearable robots)
